Meet the Team.

Launched in the Monsoon of 2025, we are one of the newer engineering teams on campus. We are organized into a smaller core team and a larger general team. The core team consists of the Team Leads. The core team meets several times a week to handle administrative tasks, plan team activities, develop technical infrastructure, and manage projects. The general team consists of all members who attend weekly sessions and club meeting.
